## 📦 Material Log
- ✅ Log material deliveries — date, expected date, material, quantity, unit, unit price, supplier
- ✅ Log material usage separately — date, material, quantity used, unit
- ✅ Bulk entry support for deliveries
- ✅ Edit and delete existing log entries

## 📊 Stock
- ✅ Live stock levels per material, calculated from deliveries minus usage

## 🏢 Suppliers
- ✅ Spend-by-supplier breakdown (entries + total spend)
- ✅ Price range comparison per material across suppliers

## 💰 Total Summary
- ✅ Project budget vs. actual spend
- ✅ Over-budget warning banner

## 📈 Visual Analytics
- ✅ Spend trend over time (cumulative)
- ✅ Cost share by material (donut + bar breakdown)
- ✅ Unit price over time (per material, selectable)
- ✅ Quantity delivered vs. used (per material)
- ✅ Supplier price comparison (per material)

## 🔔 Alerts
- ✅ Over-budget banner
- ✅ Alerts tab for project notifications

## 🔐 Access
- ✅ Admin login/logout
- ✅ Project-based access control
